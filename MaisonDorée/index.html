Our Story

Where Craft Meets Elegance

Maison Dorée was born from a singular obsession: to serve food that moves people. Chef Laurent Mercier trained in Lyon and Paris before bringing his vision to New York — a menu rooted in classical technique, elevated by seasonal ingredients and quiet innovation.

Every dish is a conversation between tradition and the present moment. We source from farms within 100 miles, pour only from small producers, and set every table as if it were the only one in the room.
